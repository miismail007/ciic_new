Centre for Prevention and Control of Corrosion in Concrete Structures

The Centre for Prevention and Control of Corrosion in Concrete Structures (PC3S) aims
  • To develop Products / Technologies / Concepts for Prevention and Control of Corrosion in Reinforced Concrete (RC) Structures.
  • To provide online / offline consultancy services for Repair and Rehabilitation of corrosion damaged buildings / structures.
  • To offer solutions for durability enhancement of newly constructed buildings / structures and service life extension of distressed buildings / structures.
  • To execute consultancy projects of Industry for validation of Products / Technologies / Concepts for corrosion prevention and control.
