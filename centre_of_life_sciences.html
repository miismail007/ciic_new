Centre for Life Sciences (CLS)

Overview

Life Sciences is one of the foremost verticals of S.T.E.M. having a great potential to boost the economy through agriculture & value added food products, food processing, healthcare and medical devices, bioremediation, cleantech, biofuels etc.. Today the potential lies beyond the Lab and Lab mouse. Technologies are developed with an aim to bring the cost of bio-based commodities within the reach of the common man. Centre of Life Science (CLS) emphasis on integrated approaches into human health, recombinant DNA technology, Internet of Other Things, Artificial Intelligence etc. The stride of frugal innovations is being stepped up across all the life sciences fields through CLS. The Centre is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and equipment that provide a stimulating environment for startups and researchers.

Centre for Life Science (CLS) is supported by BIRAC in terms of grant-in-aid of Rs.180 lakhs Ref.No: BT/BIRAC/BI-Crescent/2018 under BIONEST. We provide and support in (i) Potential cutting edge biotechnology entrepreneurs with a viable business plan, (ii) Technical assistance in the development of processes, technologies and products along with managed workspace, (iii) To connect researchers and entrepreneurs to commercialize biotech innovations and devices & eventually (iv) To increase economic and social growth of the region as well as the country.
