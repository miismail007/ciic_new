Startups:

Aligning with an incubator is the most valuable first step towards getting your startup venture off the ground.

The focus at AICDSU is to provide our startup incubatees a real-world support that goes beyond the business theories that they may have learnt at college. For the startup entrepreneur, profitability is no longer an exam question, It's rather a practical necessity that ultimately determines the viability of their idea and business model.

At AICDSU, as a startup business incubator we provide startups with valuable support and resources. Rather than being left on your own, utilise our incubation services that provide a framework for your startup, avoid the pitfalls and accelerate your growth.

If you are a startup, your next tacticle step is to contact us now.

about us

Crescent Innovation & Incubation Council (CIIC) has been established as a Section-8 not for profit company and will act as a “One Stop Shop – Technology Business Incubator (TBI)” for Start-ups. CIIC facilitates entrepreneurial & innovative ecosystem to all the stakeholders including the students, faculty, industry, investors and society at large.
